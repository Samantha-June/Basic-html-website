MY JOURNEY IN IT

Samantha was exposed to Information Technology from
a young age but was greatly exposed in her first year of varsity.
There she learnt valuable soft skills working with diverse teams
on technical projects. After varsity, she worked in social development
and realised her knack for analysis. She also identified that her previous
organisation was lacking relevant up to date data and was not adept to transform,
analyse and interpret the limited data to make meaningful and impactful decisions. It
is here that Samantha took the first step to advance her analysis skills through self-study,
internships and bootcamps-one being with the very recent GirlCode platform.
